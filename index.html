Welcome to Frolic!

Frolic! is a mobile app that allows travelers to make informed decisions about countries, cities and stays to enjoy while remaining as safe as possible in the Covid pandemic.

Hypothesis

The Covid-19 pandemic has a major impact on the travel experience because of all the restrictions and travel protocols. Travelers will also be concerned for their health and well-being.
