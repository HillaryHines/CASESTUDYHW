Welcome to Frolic!

Frolic! is a mobile app that allows travelers to make informed decisions about countries, cities and stays to enjoy while remaining as safe as possible in the Covid pandemic.

RESEARCH

Hypothesis

The Covid-19 pandemic has a major impact on the travel experience because of all the restrictions and travel protocols. Travelers will also be concerned for their health and well-being.

Protopersona

Nadine Saunders is a 36 year old executive who looks forward to her annual vacations. She loves to plan in order to have the best vacations for her with as little setbacks as possible.

Research Objectives

What factors influence Nadine’s travel? How have her travel needs changed due to the pandemic? What information does she need prior to travel and why?

Data Gathering Methods

Qualititative data gathered from 5 semi-structured interviews, quantitative data gathered from surveys.

PROBLEM

User Insight

Maria’s health is very important to her because she has a zest for life and wants to enjoy it with her loved ones to the fullest therefore she needs to be able to make decisions that maintain her and her loved ones’ safety whilst still partaking in enjoyable experiences.

Problem Statement

Current travel resources do not allow Maria to efficiently compare stays and experiences as it relates to their Covid safety measures. As a result, Maria feels apprehensive about choosing her travel experiences because she does not want herself or her loved ones to become ill.

Solution

A mobile platform that gives users information about Covid-19 for whichever location they desire. This information includes travel requirements, Covid-19 protocols, other traveler ratings of how Covid-safe stays are, and number of infections in the area.

ITERATIONS

Onboarding Iteration

Emphasized Covid-safety aspect of app during the onboarding process. Emphasized ratings on cleanliness, mask wearing as well as up to date Covid data on these locations.

Guest Ratings

Expanded guest ratings from standard room, food to include Covid safety protocols. Guests can view and/or make ratings on hand-washing, mask wearing, sanitization, and social distancing.

Covid Resources

Added hyperlinks to the locale's official health websites on every page to ensure up-to-date information on Covid burden in the area. Emphasized this feature with bold text, bright colours, and underlining.
